Memory Honey

Memory Honey came to us with an idea but no product. We spent some time helping them research a solution with customers. We had one goal, take the pain out of journaling.

We landed on a texting service that sends parents age appropriate prompts about themselves and each of their children at a convenient time each day. Their response to these prompts is automatically saved to an online journal. It is intuative & easy to use. They can print out physical books at any time. We designed and developed the product from scratch.
