Woodies Clothing

Woodies was an interesting product. It's a custom fitted male fashion brand out of NYC. There are a lot of UX challenges when you're asking regualar consumers to input their measurements. We used different user inputs and algorithms to save a personalized fit profile for each customer. This allowed us to ship them perfectly fitting pants and shirts every time. I helped woodies build their e-commerce website, and provided design work all along the way.
